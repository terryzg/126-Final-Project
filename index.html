About Our Company

At Terry and Anthony Lawn Care, we believe that a beautiful lawn should be simple and stress-free. We specialize in providing monthly lawn care services designed to keep your yard lush, green, and healthy all year round. Whether you need mowing, fertilization, weed control, or seasonal cleanups, our dedicated team delivers reliable and personalized care tailored to your property's unique needs. We proudly serve Chapel Hill, offering flexible monthly plans that make it easy for homeowners and businesses to enjoy a vibrant, well-maintained outdoor space without the hassle. Your lawn is our passion and your satisfaction is our guarantee. Let us help you create the lawn you've always dreamed of without you lifting a finger!

Serving Chapel Hill And More

Since 2000, Terry and Anthony Lawn Care LLC has been proudly serving Chapel Hill and surrounding areas with high quality landscaping solutions.
